Our Founder

Hunter Blake

Hunter is the founder of Bountiful Foods. He has enjoys having fresh and organic fruits to help with his lifestyle. He saw a great opporunity to start his own fruit farm that would help expose others to the great benefits of eating healither at an affordable cost!

At the age of just 25, he is now running a successful business that has grown over the years. He has added two food truck locations in San Clemente and Solana Beach, CA. This helps us get more exposure to additional customers that we might not get!

Our Story

We first opened back in 2018 at our main location in Carlsbad, CA. Our main location serves as a great source of fresh and locally grown produce. Inside, you'll find a store that is filled with all our amazing fruit, that is hand picked and stocked daily. We also offer a drink bar that allows customers to create their own fruit smoothie with up to 3 fruits to combine.

Our fruit smoothies is what really helped us grow and take that next step. This lead to us opening up our first food truck in Solana Beach, CA in 2019. This helped get more exposure to customers closer to San Diego and serves as a great midway point between San Diego and our main location. With business continuing to grow, we opened up our second food truck location in San Clemente, CA in 2021. This location helps us get more exposure to the Los Angeles area, where we didn't recieve many customers from.

With the expansion of our food trucks and popularity on the rise, we now sell our fruit to local farmers. We are now starting to use some national and international suppliers as well to help give even more customers access to our products! We believe everyone should experience great tasting fruit and drinks that are fresh and locally grown, as well as at an price affordable!
