Locations

Carlsbad, CA

Our HQ are located in beautiful Carlsbad, CA! Our freshly grown fruit really thrive in the amazing climate that this coastal town has to offer. Just North of San Diego, this town is just off the coast of California and offers breathtaking scenery.

With a population of arond 115,000 people, this town has a lot to offer. That is why we chose it as our location for our HQ!

Solana Beach, CA

Solana Beach is home to our first food truck! This is another great coastal town and a big reason why we chose it as our first food truck location. Located between Carlsbad and San Diego, this makes it a great place for those who live in and around San Diego to easily access or great tasting products being a midway point.

With a population of around 13,000 people, it has more of a small town vibe. We think this plays a key role for us because it allows us to get closer with the community! Our food truck is located right off the beach, which is very convenient for those who are hanging around on a hot summer day!

San Clemente, CA

San Clemente was a great location for us to open up our second food truck. Being part of Orange County, there are a lot of activities and tourist that come through this area, which we thought we be a great idea to help spread our brand.

Being just South of Los Angeles, this is another great coastal town to visit. With a population of around 65,000 people, this allows us to access a lot more customers compared to Solana Beach. We are located right off the beach at this location as well. If you are ever around, don't forget to visit the San Clemente pier that offers great views of the ocean!
